How do I create my first invoice?
Tap the "Create Invoice" button on the Home screen, select a client, add items, and tap "Generate Invoice". Your professional invoice will be ready in seconds!
Can I customize the invoice template?
Yes! Go to Settings ‚Üí Appearance to change accent colors and customize your invoice appearance to match your brand.
How do I track payments?
View all your invoices in the Home tab. Tap on any invoice to mark it as paid, pending, or overdue. Track your earnings and payment status in real-time.
Can I export invoices as PDF?
Absolutely! After creating an invoice, tap the share button to export as PDF, email directly, or share via any app on your device.
Is my data secure?
Yes, your data is stored locally on your device and synced securely via iCloud. We never share your business information with third parties.
What's included in the free version?
